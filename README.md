# ACC-RDM-Distribution-by-Year---IT-Companies
Created for the study "Asset Pricing Patterns in the U.S. Technology Sector during Periods of Abnormal Growth: Evidence from the Dot-Com and AI Eras". This repository contains the "ACC | RDM Distribution by Year - IT Companies" file, reflecting the distribution of IT companies by ACC and RDM factors between 1996 and 2024.
